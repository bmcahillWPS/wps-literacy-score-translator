How to use this tool
1
Export your results from HMH

Log into HMH and click on Assessment Report. Click "See Report" under the module assessment title. Click Export CSV.

2
Select your grade, module, and assessment type

Choose the grade level, module, and whether you are entering reading or grammar scores.

3
Upload the CSV and get your scores

Upload the file below. The tool will calculate a 1–4 score for each proficiency scale for every student — ready to enter in Empower.
